Master's degree on volumetric rendering @ Traverse Research

September 1, 2023

To finish the Game and Media Technology master’s program I obviously had to write and defend a thesis. I was very lucky to be able to do this in collaboration with Traverse Research, and work on some awesome tech. During this time I ventured into the wonderful world of volumetric rendering, with Emilio Laiso. I worked on the data structure, which ended up being largely inspired by VDB. And Emilio did the shading. Of course with plentiful collaboration when we could help each other out. During this time I also got to present my work which was an amazing opportunity.

My entire thesis can be found on github
